Run your entire short-term rental business from one place. Simplified Management is a centralized PMS that unifies listings, reservations, pricing, and OTA distribution — so property managers across India can scale from 50 to 500+ properties without the busywork.

Why a Centralized PMS

One dashboard for every listing and reservation.

Spreadsheets and a dozen extranet logins do not scale. A purpose-built property management system gives operators a single source of truth for availability, bookings, guests, and partner accounting — whether you manage vacation rentals, serviced apartments, or boutique stays.

01 / Centralize

Unified Listing Control

Create and edit every listing once. Rates, descriptions, photos, and availability flow outward to all connected OTA channels, keeping content consistent everywhere you sell.

02 / Automate

Reservations on Autopilot

New reservations land in a unified booking dashboard the moment they are made. Calendar synchronization updates availability instantly, removing the manual work that causes double bookings.

03 / Scale

Built for Growth

From a handful of apartments to a portfolio of 500+, the platform handles multi-property operations, partner payouts, and reporting without adding headcount.

How It Works

From listing to payout in three steps.

STEP 01 // CONNECT

Connect your channels

Link Airbnb, Booking.com, Agoda, Expedia, and StayVista to centralize OTA distribution under one roof.

STEP 02 // SYNC

Sync calendars in real time

Every booking updates availability across all channels automatically, eliminating double bookings before they happen.

STEP 03 // SETTLE

Track earnings and payouts

Allocate properties to partners and split earnings and expenses automatically, with clear records for every reservation.
