Why a Channel Manager

Maximize OTA distribution, eliminate overbookings.

Listing on more channels grows revenue — but only if availability stays accurate. A channel manager connects every OTA to a single inventory, pushing updates in real time so a booking on one platform instantly closes the date everywhere else.

01 / Reach

50+ OTA Connections

Distribute serviced apartments and vacation rentals across 50+ global OTA channels from a single integration layer, maximizing booking volume.

02 / Accuracy

Two-Way Sync

Inbound reservations and outbound rate and availability changes flow both ways, keeping every channel in lockstep and double bookings off the table.

03 / Control

Centralized Pricing

Set rates and restrictions once and broadcast them everywhere. Adjust pricing per channel when you need to, all from one operating dashboard.
