Partner Accounts & Split Payouts

Partner Accounts & Split Payouts for Property Investors

When several investors share a property, accounting gets messy fast. Simplified Management allocates properties to partners and splits earnings and expenses automatically — so everyone knows exactly who earns what and who pays for what, down to each daily booking.

Why Partner Payouts

Transparent partner accounting on autopilot.

Co-ownership and investor arrangements only work when the numbers are clear. The platform ties every reservation to the right partner, so revenue and operational expenses split exactly as agreed without spreadsheets or month-end disputes.

  • Track co-owner business investments per property
  • Allocate specific properties to specific partners
  • Split earnings — see who is earning what, per booking
  • Manage operational expenses — see who is paying for what
01 / Allocate

Assign Ownership

Map each property — or a share of it — to the investors behind it, with custom split ratios per partner.

02 / Split

Automatic Payouts

Every booking payment is divided by your rules, so partner payouts and expense shares are calculated automatically.

How Payouts Work

From a booking payment to a clean split.

STEP 01 // ASSIGN

Define the split

Set each partner's share of earnings and expenses per property, once.

STEP 02 // EARN

Bookings come in

Reservations and daily booking payments are recorded against the right property.

STEP 03 // SETTLE

Payouts calculate

Each partner's net earnings and expense share are computed automatically and transparently.
