Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Simplified Management collects when you submit a demo request, how we use it, and your rights over it.

Who we are

Simplified Management is a property management software and channel manager operated by B2B InDemand, based in Viman Nagar, Pune, Maharashtra, India. For any privacy question or request, contact info@simplifiedmanagement.in.

What we collect

When you submit the demo request form, we collect the information you choose to provide: your full name, work email address, the number of properties or listings you manage, and any portfolio context you enter. We do not collect this information unless you submit the form.

How we use it

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your demo request and to contact you about Simplified Management's products and services. We process this information on the basis of your consent, which you give by checking the consent box when you submit the form.

How it is handled

Form submissions are delivered to us by email through our form-processing provider (Web3Forms) and are stored in our business email inbox. We do not sell your information or share it with third parties for their own marketing.

Retention & your rights

We keep your submission for as long as needed to follow up on your enquiry and maintain our business records. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information at any time by emailing info@simplifiedmanagement.in, and we will act on your request as required by applicable law, including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always available at this page.
