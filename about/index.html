About Us

Software that ends the spreadsheet chaos of multi-property operations.

Simplified Management is a centralized property management software and channel manager for vacation rentals, serviced apartments, and boutique hotels — built for operators who run anywhere from 50 to 500+ properties.

01 / Mission

Why We Exist

Running multiple listings across Airbnb, Booking.com, and a dozen other channels means double bookings, manual calendar updates, and messy partner accounting. We exist to make that complexity disappear behind one dashboard.

02 / What We Do

The Platform

We centralize listings, synchronize calendars in real time, automate OTA distribution across 50+ channels, and split partner payouts automatically — so every booking is accounted for and nothing is double-sold.

03 / Who We Are

The Company

Simplified Management is built and supported by B2B InDemand from Viman Nagar, Pune, India, serving property businesses across India and beyond.
